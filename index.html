Sovereign Note

Introduction

The goal of the Sovereign Note project is to keep ownership of your notes and free yourself from any one particular note-taking application.

sov·​er·​eign (adjective):

acting or done independently and without outside interference

Supported Applications

As of now, Sovereign Note supports coverting between:

  • Boost Note Legacy

  • Joplin
