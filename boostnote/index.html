Boost Note

Confusingly, Boostnote.next started versioning back at 0.1.0.

Versions through 0.20.2 of Boostnote.next included an option to “Add Local Space”.

Version 0.21.0 was the first release of BoostNote.next-local, forked from BoostNote.next.
